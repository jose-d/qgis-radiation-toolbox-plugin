The original plugin was developed by OpenGeoLabs s.r.o. for the National Radiation Protection Institute (SURO), Prague, Czech Republic. It loads data from Safecast radiation monitoring devices (LOG file), including bGeigie Nano, into QGIS as a vector point layer, with experimental support for ERS 2.0 and selected PicoEnvirotec PEI files.
